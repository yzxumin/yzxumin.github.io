    Ling Xiao， University of Connecticut <\br> Title: Non-convexity of level sets of k-Hessian equations in convex ring <\br> Abstract: In this talk, we will construct explicit examples that show the sublevel sets of the solution of a k-Hessian equation defined on a convex ring do not have to be convex. This is a joint work with Zhizhang Wang.
